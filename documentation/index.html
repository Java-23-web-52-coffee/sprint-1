BrewMatch

This application provides users with a personalized way to discover local coffee shops based on their unique preferences and experiences. By allowing users to build a preference profile and privately log what they enjoy or dislike after each visit, the app continuously refines its recommendations, helping users find coffee shops that best fit their individual needs without relying solely on generic public reviews.
